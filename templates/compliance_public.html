AML/KYC Compliance

Anti-Money Laundering Framework

  • Enhanced due diligence procedures for high-risk clients
  • Continuous transaction monitoring and suspicious activity reporting
  • Regular AML training for all staff members
  • Compliance with 5th and 6th EU AML Directives
  • UK Money Laundering Regulations 2017 adherence

Know Your Customer Procedures

  • Multi-tier verification process for institutional clients
  • Beneficial ownership identification and verification
  • Ongoing monitoring and periodic reviews
  • PEP (Politically Exposed Persons) screening
  • Sanctions and watchlist screening

Security Measures

  • 256-bit AES encryption for data at rest
  • TLS 1.3 for all data in transit
  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) required
  • Regular penetration testing and security audits
  • 24/7 security operations center (SOC) monitoring
  • Incident response team with 15-minute SLA
